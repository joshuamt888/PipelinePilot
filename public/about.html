Hi, I'm Joshua Montanez. I'm a college student who fell in love with management systems and got tired of watching people lose money to organizational chaos.

Started in My Dorm Room

I'm Joshua Montanez, a college student who's weirdly obsessed with management systems. While my friends were playing video games, I was building spreadsheets for fun and studying how successful people organize their workflows.

I kept hearing the same story from everyone I talked to in sales: brilliant people losing deals because their lead tracking was a mess. Excel sheets with 47 tabs, sticky notes everywhere, important contacts buried in phone screenshots.

The existing solutions were either built for massive enterprises (way too complex) or barely better than a notepad. I realized there was a huge gap for something simple but powerful - exactly what I love building.

The Same Story Everywhere

I talked to 100+ people in sales and business development. Here's what they all complained about.

🔥

Lead Chaos

Leads scattered across 10 different platforms. No single source of truth. Hot prospects going cold while you dig through files.

💸

Money Left on Table

Forgetting to follow up. Missing opportunities. Letting warm leads turn ice cold because there's no system to track engagement.

🤯

Complex CRMs

Existing solutions are either too complicated (hello, Salesforce) or too basic. You need something that just works, not a PhD to operate.

My Solution

A lead management system that actually makes sense. Simple enough for anyone to use, powerful enough to transform how you do business.

🎯

Dead Simple Organization

Three categories: Cold, Warm, CRM. That's it. No complex pipelines, no overwhelming features. Just organize leads the way your brain naturally thinks.

🚀

Built for Speed

Add a lead in 10 seconds. Update status in 2 clicks. I obsessed over every interaction to make sure nothing gets in your way when you're in the zone.

📈

Revenue-Focused

Every feature exists to help you close more deals. Goal tracking, source analytics, conversion insights - all designed to turn your hustle into cash.

My Mission

To help driven people turn their hustle into organized success. I believe that great ideas and hard work shouldn't be wasted because of bad systems. Every ambitious person deserves tools that amplify their efforts instead of getting in their way.
